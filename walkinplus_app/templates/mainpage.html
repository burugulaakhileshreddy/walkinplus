About WalkIn+

WalkIn+ was built for small and mid-size businesses in India that still depend on paper registers to track who walked in. We started with clinics and quickly realised the same problem exists in salons, coaching centers, repair shops, labs and many other walk-in businesses.

Our goal is simple – give you a clean, fast and affordable tool that does just one thing really well: capture and manage walk-ins without any extra complexity.

No heavy POS. No confusing dashboards. Just a smart, searchable walk-in register that lives in your browser.
